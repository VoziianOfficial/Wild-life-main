Our Role

We help you describe your concern clearly.

Lacerta is designed to help homeowners describe their wildlife concern clearly while keeping provider choice, pricing, methods, and hiring responsibility transparent.

What we do

Help homeowners create a clearer request.

  • Collect wildlife concern type, location, urgency, and property details.
  • Route requests toward independent local provider options where available.
  • Make it easier to compare providers before choosing who to contact.
What we do not do

We are not the provider that performs the work.

  • We do not remove, trap, inspect, clean up, exclude, or repair wildlife issues directly.
  • We do not set provider prices, response times, warranties, or service methods.
  • We do not replace homeowner verification of license, insurance, and terms.
