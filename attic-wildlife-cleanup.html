Questions to ask before choosing a cleanup provider.

What cleanup includes

Ask whether odor, droppings, nesting material, or sanitizing work is included.

Attic or crawlspace scope

Ask whether the provider handles the relevant area and what access is needed.

Related exclusion

Ask whether exclusion or entry-point work is included, referred, or separate.

Licensing and insurance
