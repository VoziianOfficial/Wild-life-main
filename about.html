Platform purpose

Built for clarity when the situation feels unclear.

Wildlife concerns can be urgent, unfamiliar, and difficult to compare. Lacerta exists to give homeowners a clearer first step: submit the concern, see available provider options where possible, and review details before deciding what to do next.

Lacerta is independent. We are not a wildlife removal company, contractor, pest control company, trapping company, or inspection company.

Mission

Help homeowners ask better provider questions.

The platform is designed around comparison, not contractor claims. Lacerta can help collect request details, identify relevant provider categories, and encourage homeowners to ask about pricing, methods, licensing, insurance, cleanup scope, and local regulations.

  • Clear separation between Lacerta and participating providers.
  • Plain-language reminders about what homeowners should verify.
  • Service category pages written without direct removal promises.

Provider questions

A better comparison starts with direct questions.

Ask how pricing is determined.

Providers may consider animal type, access, location, timing, cleanup scope, and follow-up needs. Request written details before authorizing work.

Ask about methods and local rules.

Methods and timing can vary by species and location. Providers should explain the approach they propose and any local regulation considerations.

Ask for documentation.

Homeowners should verify licensing, insurance, and any required permits directly with the provider they choose.

Ask what cleanup includes.

Clarify whether odor, droppings, nesting material, insulation, sanitizing, or exclusion-related work is included, optional, or separate.

Ask what warranty language means.

Warranty terms are provided by participating providers. Review time limits, exclusions, follow-up process, and written service terms.
