Provider questions

A better comparison starts with direct questions.

Ask how pricing is determined.

Providers may consider animal type, access, location, timing, cleanup scope, and follow-up needs. Request written details before authorizing work.

Ask about methods and local rules.

Methods and timing can vary by species and location. Providers should explain the approach they propose and any local regulation considerations.

Ask for documentation.

Homeowners should verify licensing, insurance, and any required permits directly with the provider they choose.

Ask what cleanup includes.

Clarify whether odor, droppings, nesting material, insulation, sanitizing, or exclusion-related work is included, optional, or separate.

Ask what warranty language means.

Warranty terms are provided by participating providers. Review time limits, exclusions, follow-up process, and written service terms.
