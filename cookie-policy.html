Cookies and browser storage

Lacerta uses browser storage, including localStorage, to remember your cookie banner choice. This prevents the banner from reappearing after you accept or decline.

Essential storage may also support basic website functionality and request-form operation.

Consent banner storage

When you click Accept or Decline, the website stores a simple consent value in your browser. The banner does not disappear until a choice is made.

Optional analytics

If optional analytics or performance tools are added in the future, they should be disclosed and used consistently with this Cookie Policy and the Privacy Policy.

Managing storage choices

You can clear cookies and localStorage through your browser settings. Clearing browser storage may cause the consent banner to appear again.

Independent provider-matching model

Lacerta helps homeowners submit wildlife concerns and compare local provider options. Lacerta does not perform wildlife removal directly. Participating providers set their own availability, pricing, methods, warranties, and service terms.
