Independent provider-matching platform

Lacerta helps homeowners submit wildlife concerns and compare available local provider options. Lacerta does not perform wildlife removal, trapping, inspection, cleanup, or repair services directly.

Information we collect

When you submit a request, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, selected service category, source page, message details, and any property-context information you choose to provide.

We may also collect limited technical information such as browser storage consent choices and basic request metadata needed to operate the website.

How information is used

  • To receive and process your wildlife concern request.
  • To help identify relevant local provider categories or participating provider options.
  • To communicate with you about your request.
  • To maintain website security, consent preferences, and basic operational records.

Provider matching and possible provider contact

Lacerta may share request details with participating providers so they can discuss availability, pricing, methods, warranties, and service terms directly with you. Provider options vary by location and concern type.

Homeowners are responsible for verifying licensing, insurance, methods, local regulations, and provider terms before authorizing any work.

Cookies and localStorage

The website uses browser storage to remember cookie consent choices. If optional analytics or performance tools are added, they should be used consistently with this policy and the Cookie Policy.

Data security basics

We use reasonable safeguards designed to protect request information. No website, email system, or provider-matching process can guarantee absolute security.

Your choices

You may choose not to submit a request through the website. You can also clear browser storage to remove cookie consent preferences. To contact Lacerta about privacy questions, email hello@lacertamatch.com.
